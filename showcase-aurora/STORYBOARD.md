# Chasing Light — Storyboard

One character, one night, four scenes. Mira chases the aurora across an arctic
plain while the sky transforms around her — proof that the *same* person and
wardrobe hold across wildly different cinematic shots. Music-driven, no
voiceover; the visuals carry it. ~24s, four 6s beats.

Palette arc: starlit blue → aurora teal-green → violet → dawn amber.
Camera language: slow, deliberate, anamorphic. No fast cuts, no gimmicks.
